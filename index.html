We are an indie game team who want to give our players large and fun games to explore. Our team consists of Ari Gibson, William Pellen and Jack Vine. We have created Hollow Knight and are currently creating our next game called Hollow Knight Silksong. The goal with our games are to challenge the player while still giving you a fun experience. On this website you can find infomration about our games, some FAQ about our games and upcoming games and you can find some information about us the team.
